# Creedon's Health Cholesterol

## What This App Does
Helps patients track and manage cholesterol via healthy eating and activities.

## How We Use Health Data
This app pulls labs and vitals from a patient's MyChart.

## Who This App Is For
Patients and communities.

## Privacy Policy
We take privacy seriously. Data is only used for the purposes described above and is never sold or shared with third parties.
